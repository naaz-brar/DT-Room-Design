DESIGN CHOICES

I kept my room design simple because I wanted it to feel calm and relaxing. I used mostly blue and white colours because they look clean and peaceful together. To make the room more interesting, I added small bits of pink. For example, you can see pink pop out of some books, lamps and my water bottle. These additional colour make my room more vibrant without it being too much.
